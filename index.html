About the Book

In this refreshingly crude, “keeping it real” how-to guide, Author and Master Connector, Robin Wheeler brazenly, but lovingly chips away at all your “how not to be great” excuses and gives you the tools to help you chisel out your sexiest, best self-sculpture. These sometimes rude but ALWAYS relevant, life-changing chapters are full of transforming analogies such as: PEACE OF MIND OVER A PIECE OF MAN… UNAPOLOGETIC ATTITUDE… And other thought provoking, ass kicking concepts that will help you reclaim your PEACE OF MIND, identify and transform self-sabotaging beliefs and relationships that keep you running on the hamster wheel of ‘love’ and hinder you from finding and developing the relationships you really want. By the end of TAKE BACK YOUR P*SSY you will have a powerful sense of self, peace of mind and a forgiving but unwavering commitment to who you are and who you aspire to be! TAKE BACK YOUR P*SSY!
